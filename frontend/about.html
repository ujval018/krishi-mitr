About Krishi-Mitr

Our Mission

Krishi-Mitr is dedicated to empowering farmers by providing a platform for crop bartering and reselling. We aim to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem where farmers can exchange goods and services without the need for traditional currency.

How It Works

  1. List your available crops for barter or resale
  2. Browse crops offered by other farmers
  3. Connect and negotiate trades
  4. Complete transactions securely
